About The Project.

In this project for my MIS 15 class we had to develop a Windows Form application that determined what an employee would be paid depending on

  • The type of employee
  • The hours worked
  • Their Sales Revenue

Action

After taking the requirements into consideration I created an application that took the information that the user entered and calculated the money earned through sales commission and the total pay that the employee would receive based on the data entered by the user.

Exceptions and Try-Catch Statements

I also added exception handlers to stop the user from

  • Leaving data blank
  • Entering invalid data
